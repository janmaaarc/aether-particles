Welcome to AETHER!

Let's learn how to control the particles with your hands

1

Allow Camera Access

We need camera access to track your hand movements. Click "Allow" when prompted.

2

Open & Close Your Hand

Open your hand to expand particles outward. Close your fist to contract them to the center.

3

Pinch Gesture

Touch your thumb and index finger together to create an implosion effect with enhanced glow.

4

Fast Movement

Move your hand quickly to create turbulence - particles will shake chaotically!

5

Two Hands

Use both hands for spread and compress gestures. Move hands apart to expand, together to compress.
